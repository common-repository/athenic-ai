## Description

Athenic AI is a powerful data analysis tool that integrates seamlessly with your WooCommerce store. Measure important E-Commerce metrics like Lifetime-Value, Price Elasticity, and more.

The plugin adds a new menu item to your WooCommerce dashboard, which opens the Athenic AI app. The app connects to your store automatically, providing you with a comprehensive analysis of your store's data.

Key features:

* Easy installation and setup.
* Seamless integration with WooCommerce.
* Automatic connection to your store.
* Comprehensive data analysis using Athenic AI.

## Installation

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/athenic-ai` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
3. Opening the plugin from your dashboard will automatically configure Athenic AI.



## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is Athenic AI?

Athenic AI is a powerful data analysis tool that uses artificial intelligence and machine learning to analyze data and provide valuable insights.

### How does Athenic AI integrate with my WooCommerce store?

The Athenic AI plugin adds a new menu item to your WooCommerce dashboard. When you click on this menu item, it opens the Athenic AI app, which connects to your store automatically and analyzes your store.

### What kind of data does Athenic AI analyze?

Athenic AI can analyze all the data in your WooCommerce store, including sales data, customer data, product data, and more.

### Is my data safe with Athenic AI?

Yes, your data is safe with Athenic AI. The plugin only uses your data for analysis and does not share it with any third parties.
View https://www.athenic.com/security for more details.
